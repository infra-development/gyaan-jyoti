# Finance Mastery Curriculum - Knowledge Map

This document serves as the master checklist for the entire five-year curriculum.

Every finance concept taught during the curriculum must appear here.

No major topic should be omitted.

Each topic progresses through four levels:

* Level 1 — Introduction
* Level 2 — Understanding
* Level 3 — Application
* Level 4 — Mastery
