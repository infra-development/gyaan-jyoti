# Year 4 – Module Plan

This document breaks Year 4 into 10 monthly modules.

Each module consists of **4 weeks**.

Each week will later be divided into daily lessons.

---

# Module 1 — Corporate Finance Fundamentals

**Weeks:** 1–4

### Goal

Understand how companies raise, use, and manage money.

### Week 1

* Why companies need money
* Sources of capital

### Week 2

* Debt vs Equity

### Week 3

* Capital structure
* Cost of capital (concept)

### Week 4

* Corporate finance case study
* Review
* Module project
