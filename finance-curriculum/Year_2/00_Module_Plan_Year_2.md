# Year 2 – Module Plan

This document breaks Year 2 into 10 monthly modules.

Each module consists of **4 weeks**.

Each week will later be divided into daily lessons.

---

# Module 1 — Modern Banking

**Weeks:** 1–4

### Goal

Understand how modern banks work and why they are important.

**Week 1**

* History of banks
* Why banks exist

**Week 2**

* Savings accounts
* Current accounts
* Deposits

**Week 3**

* ATMs
* Debit cards
* Internet banking

**Week 4**

* Bank visit / virtual banking activity
* Review
* Module project
