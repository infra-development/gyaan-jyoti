# Year 1 – Module Plan

This document breaks Year 1 into 10 monthly modules.

Each module consists of **4 weeks**.

Each week will later be divided into daily lessons.

---

# Module 1 — What Is Money?

**Weeks:** 1–4

## Goal

Introduce the concept of money and why it exists.

### Week 1

* Life before money
* Why humans trade
* Problems without money

### Week 2

* Barter system
* Advantages
* Disadvantages

### Week 3

* Birth of money
* Different forms of money
* Coins and currency

### Week 4

* Why people trust money
* Review
* Module project

---
